## 1. The Decision

**No free plan.** **Pro at $49/mo** includes 500 documents/month with $0.15/doc overage. **Business at $79/mo** includes 1,000 documents/month with $0.10/doc overage. **Enterprise** is custom-quoted with unlimited documents and no overage. Exceeding your monthly limit does NOT stop your workflow -- extra documents are charged at the overage rate automatically.

## 4. Overage Rules

- When a user exceeds their monthly limit (e.g., document #501 on Pro), the system does NOT stop.
- Extra documents are charged at the overage rate automatically.
- Example: Pro user uploads 600 docs in a month -> $49 + (100 x $0.15) = $49 + $15 = $64 total.
- Enterprise: unlimited documents, no overage fees.

**Justification:**

- **Pro at $49/mo** positions Vertifile as a professional-grade tool, not a consumer app. At 500 docs/month with $0.15 overage, a heavy user uploading 600 docs pays $64 -- still competitive with DocuSign and other verification platforms.
- **Business at $79/mo** is a flat rate (no per-seat), making it simple for teams. 1,000 docs/month with $0.10 overage handles most small-to-mid firm volumes.
- **Enterprise** is custom-quoted for white-label, on-prem, SSO with custom IdP, custom legal opinion, dedicated CSM, or any deal needing a security questionnaire response.

---

## 5. Why Someone Upgrades

**Pro -> Business.** The trigger is **scale and team**. A Pro user at 500 docs/month starts paying overage, and the jump to Business at $79/mo with 1,000 docs and $0.10 overage becomes economically obvious. The moment they also say "I want my paralegal to also issue PVFs," the team features close the deal.

**Business -> Enterprise.** The trigger is **procurement**. Once a buyer asks for a custom MSA, security questionnaire, named CSM, or on-prem deployment, they are an Enterprise account. Vendor-management policy at firms above ~200 lawyers will not allow self-serve checkout for a tool in the document chain of custody.

## 6. Revenue Model

At **200 Pro ($49/mo) + 50 Business ($79/mo) + 20 Enterprise (avg $3K/mo)** = roughly **$73.8K MRR / $885K ARR**. With overage revenue from heavy users, realistically $80K-90K MRR. The fastest path is 50 design-partner law/accounting firms acquired through targeted founder-led sales.
