- Keep under 60 characters when possible (YouTube truncates at ~60 on mobile)

## Thumbnail Text Ideas

Thumbnails should have minimal text (3-5 words max), high contrast, and a clear visual.

### Text Options
1. **"REAL or FAKE?"** -- split screen green stamp vs red stamp
2. **"9 LAYERS"** -- over a visualization of the security stack
3. **"FORGED"** -- red stamp with X, alarming feel
4. **"VERIFIED"** -- green stamp with checkmark, clean feel
5. **"$600B PROBLEM"** -- over a pile of documents
6. **"STOP FRAUD"** -- bold text, red and white
7. **"IS IT REAL?"** -- over a diploma or contract close-up

### Thumbnail Design Rules
- Use 1280x720 resolution (16:9)
- Text must be readable at mobile size (thumbnail appears at ~120px wide)
- Maximum 2 colors for text (white + one accent)
- Use the Vertifile green (#00C853 or similar) and red (#FF1744) for verified/forged contrast
- No small text, no clutter, no logos smaller than 10% of frame
- Face + text outperforms text-only (use the founder or a team member when possible)
- A/B test thumbnails using YouTube Studio if the channel has enough traffic

## End Screen CTA Suggestions

End screens appear in the last 20 seconds. YouTube allows up to 4 elements.

### Recommended Layout (last 20 seconds)

1. **Subscribe button** -- always include
2. **Next video card** -- link to the most relevant related video
3. **Playlist card** -- link to the "Vertifile Explained" playlist
4. **External link** -- vertifile.com (requires YouTube Partner Program)

### Verbal CTAs (voiceover in the last 15 seconds)
- "If you want to see how Vertifile catches a forged document, watch this next."
- "Subscribe for more on document security -- we post new videos every week."
- "Try Vertifile free right now -- link in the description."
- "Watch our full 3-minute explainer to see all 9 security layers in action."

### End Screen Design
- Keep the visual background simple so the clickable elements stand out
- Avoid placing critical content behind the end screen cards
- Use a consistent end screen template across all videos for brand recognition
- Always include a verbal CTA -- viewers are more likely to click when prompted
