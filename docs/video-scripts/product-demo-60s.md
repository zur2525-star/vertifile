# Vertifile Product Demo -- 60 Seconds

**Format:** Product demo / social ad
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Tone:** Urgent hook, clean visuals, confident close
**Resolution:** 1920x1080 (landscape) + 1080x1920 (vertical cut)

---

## Scene 1 -- Hook (0:00 - 0:08)

**Visual:** Dark background. White text fades in word by word. Cut to montage of news headlines about document fraud -- diplomas, contracts, invoices.

**Voiceover:**
"Every year, millions of documents are forged. Diplomas. Contracts. Medical records. And right now, there is no way to know which ones are real."

**Text on screen:** MILLIONS OF DOCUMENTS FORGED EVERY YEAR

**Sound:** Low tension drone, newspaper shuffle SFX

---

## Scene 2 -- Upload (0:08 - 0:20)

**Visual:** Clean screen recording of vertifile.com. User drags a PDF into the upload zone. Progress bar fills smoothly. The system processes the file and generates a .pvf output.

**Voiceover:**
"Vertifile changes that. Upload any PDF to vertifile.com. In seconds, it becomes a PVF -- a protected, verifiable file that carries proof of its origin."

**Text on screen:** PDF --> PVF in seconds

**Sound:** Soft UI clicks, subtle whoosh on completion

---

## Scene 3 -- Verification (0:20 - 0:35)

**Visual:** Recipient opens the PVF file. The Vertifile stamp appears and spins -- lands on GREEN with a checkmark. Security layers cascade briefly on screen (hash, timestamp, metadata).

**Voiceover:**
"When someone opens that PVF, Vertifile runs nine independent security checks. If everything matches -- the stamp turns green. This proves it is real. Untampered. Authentic."

**Text on screen:** 9 SECURITY LAYERS -- VERIFIED

**Sound:** Satisfying confirmation chime

---

## Scene 4 -- Forgery Detection (0:35 - 0:48)

**Visual:** Side-by-side comparison. On the left, the verified PVF (green). On the right, a forged copy is opened. The stamp animation freezes. Turns RED. A bold X appears over the stamp.

**Voiceover:**
"But change even a single byte? The stamp freezes red. No ambiguity. No guessing. Forgery detected instantly."

**Text on screen:** TAMPERED -- VERIFICATION FAILED

**Sound:** Sharp alert tone, glass crack SFX

---

## Scene 5 -- CTA (0:48 - 1:00)

**Visual:** Vertifile logo animates center screen. URL appears below. Brief shot of the upload interface on desktop and mobile.

**Voiceover:**
"Stop trusting documents blindly. Try Vertifile free at vertifile.com."

**Text on screen:**
- TRY FREE AT VERTIFILE.COM
- Vertifile logo
- Tagline: "Trust starts here."

**Sound:** Uplifting resolve, clean fade out

---

## Production Notes

- Screen recordings should use a clean browser window, no bookmarks bar
- Use real PVF files for authenticity -- do not fake the UI
- Green stamp animation must match the actual product behavior
- Red stamp freeze should feel abrupt and alarming -- contrast with the smooth green flow
- Vertical cut (9:16) for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- Subtitles required on all versions (80% of social video is watched on mute)
