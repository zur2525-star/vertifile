# Vertifile Demo Video Script (60 seconds)

## Equipment Needed
- Screen recording software (QuickTime, Loom, or OBS)
- Microphone
- Browser open to vertifile.com

## Script

### Scene 1 (0-10s) — The Problem
[Show a PDF diploma on screen]
VOICEOVER: "Every year, millions of documents are forged — diplomas, medical records, contracts. And there's no way to know if what you're looking at is real."

### Scene 2 (10-25s) — The Solution
[Navigate to vertifile.com/upload, drag a PDF]
VOICEOVER: "Vertifile changes that. Upload any document, and in seconds it becomes a tamper-proof .pvf file with a live holographic stamp."
[Show the PVF being created with progress steps]

### Scene 3 (25-40s) — Verification
[Open the .pvf file, show the spinning stamp]
VOICEOVER: "The stamp continuously rotates — proving the document is live and unaltered. If anyone changes even a single character..."
[Show a forged version with frozen stamp and red X]
VOICEOVER: "...the stamp freezes, turns red, and marks it as FORGED."

### Scene 4 (40-55s) — Technology
[Show the verification page with green checkmark]
VOICEOVER: "Behind the scenes: SHA-256 hashing, HMAC signatures, blockchain anchoring — and your document content is never read by our servers."

### Scene 5 (55-60s) — CTA
[Show vertifile.com homepage]
VOICEOVER: "Try it free at vertifile.com. Make document forgery impossible."
[Logo + tagline]
