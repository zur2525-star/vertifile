## General Production Notes

- **First 2 seconds are everything.** If the hook doesn't stop the scroll, nothing else matters. Every script above leads with conflict, curiosity, or a visual surprise.
- **Captions are mandatory.** 80%+ of TikTok/Reels viewers watch without sound. Every script should work as text-overlay-only.
- **Authenticity beats production value.** Founder-to-camera with natural lighting outperforms polished corporate content on these platforms.
- **Post timing:** Weekdays 7-9 AM and 5-7 PM local time for professional audiences. Weekends for educational content.
- **Cross-post strategy:** Every TikTok becomes a Reel becomes a YouTube Short. Adjust aspect ratio and remove TikTok watermark before reposting.
- **Engagement bait (ethical):** End with questions. "Would you trust a document without this?" drives comments. Pin a comment with additional context.
