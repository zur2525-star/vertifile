How PVF Protection Works

Every PVF file is a self-contained proof of authenticity that works anywhere, anytime.

Your document is hashed — we never see its content. Only a unique fingerprint is computed.

A cryptographic signature proves authenticity. Any tampering is instantly detectable.

The PVF file is self-contained — it works offline. No internet needed to view or verify.
