Every Credential Verifiable by Any Employer

Employers verify any diploma or transcript in seconds by opening the .pvf file in any browser. No special software, no account needed, no calls to the registrar's office.

Cryptographic verification tied to your institution's identity makes it impossible for diploma mills to produce credentials that pass verification. Every .pvf file traces back to the issuing institution.
