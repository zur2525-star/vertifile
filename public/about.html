Our Mission

Why We Built Vertifile

Document fraud is a global crisis hiding in plain sight.

Every year, millions of forged documents circulate worldwide — fake university diplomas, altered medical records, fraudulent legal contracts. The damage is enormous: unqualified professionals in critical roles, insurance fraud costing billions, and individuals whose identities are stolen through forged paperwork.

Vertifile was born from a simple realization: if we can make currency nearly impossible to counterfeit, why can't we do the same for documents? We built a cryptographic verification system that makes any document tamper-proof while keeping your content completely private.

Privacy by Design

BLIND Processing

Your content stays yours. Always. Our server never reads a single word.

Client-Side Hashing

Your document is hashed locally in the browser using SHA-256. The file content never leaves your device.

Fingerprint Only

Only the one-way cryptographic fingerprint is sent to our servers. It is mathematically impossible to reverse.

Blockchain Sealed

The hash is registered on blockchain, creating an immutable proof of authenticity. Zero knowledge of your content.

Technology

Built on Open Standards

Vertifile is establishing a new standard for document verification.

Patent Pending

The PVF format and verification process are patent-pending with the Israeli Patent Office, establishing a new standard for document protection.

IANA MIME Type

We submitted the first IANA MIME type registration for document verification (application/pvf), so .pvf files are recognized by browsers and operating systems worldwide.

Blockchain Anchoring

Every document hash is permanently recorded on Polygon blockchain, creating an immutable, publicly auditable trail that exists forever.
