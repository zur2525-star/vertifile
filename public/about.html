About Vertifile

Making document verification accessible, secure, and privacy-first for everyone.

Our Mission

Ending Document Forgery

We believe every document deserves to be trusted.

Vertifile was created with a singular purpose: to make document verification accessible, secure, and privacy-first. In a world where digital documents can be easily manipulated, we provide a cryptographic proof of authenticity that anyone can verify instantly.

Our technology ensures that diplomas, medical records, legal contracts, and official documents can be trusted without question. We do this while maintaining absolute privacy through our BLIND processing architecture.

How It Works

BLIND Processing

Your content stays yours. Always.

We Never See Your Content

When you upload a document to Vertifile, our system computes a SHA-256 cryptographic hash entirely on your device. Only this one-way fingerprint is sent to our servers. It is mathematically impossible to reconstruct any document content from its hash. Your files, your data, your privacy.

Our Values

What We Stand For

The principles that guide everything we build.

Privacy First

We architected our entire system so we never have access to your document content. Privacy is not a feature — it is the foundation.

Security

Eight layers of cryptographic protection, blockchain anchoring, and live anti-forgery stamps make document tampering impossible.

Transparency

Every verification is publicly auditable on the blockchain. Our process is open, our records are immutable, and our integrity is verifiable.

Accessibility

Anyone can verify a document — no account needed, no software to install. Just open a .pvf file in any browser, anywhere in the world.

Our Story

Built in Israel, for the World

Founded in 2026 to solve document forgery at scale.

Vertifile was founded in 2026 in Israel by a team of security and cryptography experts who saw first-hand the damage caused by document forgery — from fake diplomas to forged medical records and fraudulent contracts.

We built a solution that combines cryptographic signing, blockchain immutability, and live holographic stamps to create a new standard for document trust. Our BLIND processing architecture ensures that privacy is never compromised.
